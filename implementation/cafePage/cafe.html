Our Cafe

Visit Our Cafe

After a long day of petting and feeding animals, you might get hungry or thirsty, which is exactly why we created a fantastic cafe for anyone to drink and eat their fill. We sell a variety of different teas and coffees but if that's not your style, we also sell other drinks such as hot chocolate, apple/orange juice (homemade of course), or even some milk.


If you're not looking for a drink but instead wish for something to eat, then check out our menu. We sell a variety of biscuits, cookies and cake including (but not limited to, chocolate, strawberry, banana, and carrot). If this doesn't already sound too good to be true, then how about our family deal? If you come in a group of 3 or more people, then we will knock off a whole 25% from your total as a thank you for your visit.
