Fun Farms

About Us

Fun Farms is a small and fun farm with lots of animals, food to sell, lots of facilities to check out, and lots of fun to be had. We are so dedicated to the fun that you and your family can have here that we decided to put it in our name. We have plenty for you on offer! Check out our cafe with delicious tea and coffee, our petting zoo for you to feed and pet the animals to your hearts content, and our amazing play park!


Fun Farms is for anyone who loves animals, who loves fun, or anyone who just wants a little break and pet some animals. If any of this sounds good to you, come and check us out. Our team is ready to welcome you and promise you a fabulous and memorable day out with lots of interesting facts about our very special animals. Our newly built play park is a fun and safe enviroment for your kids to explore and our cafe is the perfect place to recharge.


(Meadowgreen Farmhouse is another name we go by)


What We Offer

Fun Farms sells a variety of produce. Some of our most popular produce includes:

  • Our Super Sweet Strawberries
  • Our Cosmic Crisp Apples
  • Our Really Ripe Bananas
And many more!


Not only do we sell fresh produce, we also have a cafe, a petting zoo and a play park for kids,
so when you visit us, you won't get bored!
