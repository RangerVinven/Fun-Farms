Kid's Play Park

Kid's Play Park

If you're bringing your kids to our farm, then they will love our new kid's play park where they can not only pet the animals but actually get to play with the animals and feed them. You may be concerned with the saftey of this service, but I can assure you that it is 100% safe. Our highly qualified and trained staff will be right there talking to the children about what not to do and answering any questions that they may have.


If your kids love the petting zoo, then they're going to love the play park. By bringing your kids here, you're giving them a healthy bond and connection with animals and nature which could make them decide that they would like to have a career in dealing or researching animals, by bringing them here, you could be introducing them to their dream future.
