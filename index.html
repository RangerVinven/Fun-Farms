Fun Farms

About Us

Fun Farms is a small farm but fun farm with lots of food to sell, lots of facilities to check out, and lots of fun to be had. We are so dedicated to the fun that you and your family can have here that we decided to put it in our name. Now you may be wondering what you could actually do here if you decide to come, well there's plenty for you to do! Some of our most popular facilities includes our cafe with delicious tea and coffee, our petting zoo for you to pet the animals to your hearts content, and if that's not enough fun for you, then how about the kid's play park where they not only get to pet the animals, but play with them and feed them as well!


Fun Farms is for anyone who loves animals, who loves fun, or anyone who just wants a little break from the outside world and would like pet some animals. If you match any of these points, then I would recommed you come along and check us out, you won't regret it!
