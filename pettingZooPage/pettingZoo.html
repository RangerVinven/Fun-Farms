Visit Our Petting Zoo

We have a wide variety of animals for you and your friends/family to get to know and pet. Say hello to our cows, pigs, goats, chickens, turkeys, peacocks, rabbits, horses and our most cuddly koala bears! We have plenty of food and friendly staff that will help you feed our animals and make sure everbody stays safe. Another thing we have to offer is our free photographer that will capture your time at our farm.


So what are you waiting for? We hope to see you soon and promise you an exciting day out. Meet, feed, pet and learn about our animals, have some food and drinks, spend some time in the play park, and most importantly make memories.
