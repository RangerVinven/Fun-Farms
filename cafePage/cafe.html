Our Cafe

Visit Our Cafe

After a long day of petting and feeding animals, you might get hungry or thirsty as well, which is exactly why we decided to create a fantastic cafe for anyone to drink and eat their fill. We sell a variety of different teas and coffies but if that's not your style, we also sell other kinds of drinks such as hot chocolate, apple/orange juice (homemade of course), or even some milk.


If you're not looking for a drink but instead wish for something to eat, then we also sell food. We sell the classic biscuits and cookies and some cake including (but not limited to, chocolate, strawberry, banana, and carrot) if you wish. What ever you crave, you will have! If it already doesn't sound too good to be true, then how about our family deal? If you come in a group of 3 or more people, then we will knock off a whole 25% from your total as a thank you for you deciding to come to our farm.

Our Menu
